Overview

Chennai Public School likes to tap all possible opportunities to give its students a variety in its curriculum. Cambridge English offered by the University of Cambridge, UK is one such opportunity designed to promote English amongst speakers of other languages. It is mandatory for all Chennai Public School students to take up the Cambridge English courses according to their age groups.

A good knowledge of English, backed up by the right English language qualification, can give individuals a head start in life, leading to exciting study, work and life opportunities at home and abroad. Cambridge English exams are officially accepted by thousands of universities, employers and governments globally which will open doors for students to study or work abroad in the future.
